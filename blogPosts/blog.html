How I became a software developer?

September 2, 2014

Hello everyone! Or anyone?

How I became a Software Developer, you ask. Well, I am still working on that.

I am someone who did not know how to code. No previous coding experience or knowledge. None. Nada. Zero. Sifir. I guess, that covers my background in Computer Science. So far so good, yeah? I decided to learn how to code because... Well, that is another blog post all together.

Hmmm. Where to start though? Those were my thoughts exactly. There were/are so many resources available online, it is borderline between exciting and frustrating. As many self taught programmers out there, I started with Codecademy tutorials. There was also a Coursera class on Python. Then I learned about other online and free resources and started playing with them little by little. I was living in a small town at the time and there were no coding schools or people that were changing careers to open my mind to the possibility of becoming a software developer.

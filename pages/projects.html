Netflix and chill? So 2015. This is better. This web app gets its videos from Instagram users that posted videos using #hyperlapse and its music from SoundCloud. Written in JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5, and CSS3, utilizing Instagram and SoundCloud APIs. Go on now.

Free Fall

A web app (wannabe simulation) that imitates free falling based on different gravities of different planets. And no Moon is not a planet but we will be super nice and include him. Yes moon, you can sit with us. Written in JavaScript, using jQuery, HTML5, and CSS3.

RepIt

A web app that replays music videos of your obsessive compulsive mind's choice mimicing a replay button. So yes, it replays them forever so you don't have to. Just sit back and obsess over something else. Written in JavaScript, using jQuery, HTML5, and CSS3. It utilizes Youtube API.
